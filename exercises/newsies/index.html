Students Survive Week 1 at V School

Despite snow, students arrived (and kept arriving) on campus three days this week, staying awake using caffeine and the sheer force of will. Some of them even learned things they didn't already know.

Many students were delighted by the way the building shakes, making them feel like they actually were in Silicon Valley, not Silicon Slopes.

Also much appreciated was the lovely view out the south window, which showcases a Carl's Jr. with a drive-through. A random poll of several students suggested that this alone was worth the tuition money they paid to come here for the next twelve weeks.
